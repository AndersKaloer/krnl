15 Timer0:
16 - Timer0 is a 8bit timer.
17 - In the Arduino world Timer0 is been used for the timer functions, like delay(), millis() and micros().
18 - If you change Timer0 registers, this may influence the Arduino timer function.
19 - So you should know what you are doing.
20 
21 Timer1:
22 - Timer1 is a 16bit timer.
23 - In the Arduino world the Servo library uses Timer1 on Arduino Uno (Timer5 on Arduino Mega).
24 
25 Timer2:
26 - Timer2 is a 8bit timer like Timer0.
27  -In the Arduino work the tone() function uses Timer2.
28 
29 Timer3, Timer4, Timer5: Timer 3,4,5 are only available on Arduino Mega boards.
30 - These timers are all 16bit timers.
31 
32 
33 On uno
34 - Pins 5 and 6: controlled by timer0
35 - Pins 9 and 10: controlled by timer1
36 - Pins 11 and 3: controlled by timer2
37 
38 On the Arduino Mega we have 6 timers and 15 PWM outputs:
39 
40 - Pins 4 and 13: controlled by timer0
41 - Pins 11 and 12: controlled by timer1
42 - Pins 9 and10: controlled by timer2
43 - Pin 2, 3 and 5: controlled by timer 3
44 - Pin 6, 7 and 8: controlled by timer 4
45 - Pin 46, 45 and 44:: controlled by timer 5
46 
47 ... from http://arduino-info.wikispaces.com/Timers-Arduino
48 
49 - Servo Library uses Timer1.
50 -- You can’t use PWM on Pin 9, 10 when you use the Servo Library on an Arduino.
51 -- For Arduino Mega it is a bit more difficult. The timer needed depends on the number of servos.
52 -- Each timer can handle 12 servos.
53 -- For the first 12 servos timer 5 will be used (losing PWM on Pin 44,45,46).
54 -- For 24 Servos timer 5 and 1 will be used (losing PWM on Pin 11,12,44,45,46)..
55 -- For 36 servos timer 5, 1 and 3 will be used (losing PWM on Pin 2,3,5,11,12,44,45,46)..
56 -- For 48 servos all 16bit timers 5,1,3 and 4 will be used (losing all PWM pins).
57 
58 - Pin 11 has shared functionality PWM and MOSI.
59 -- MOSI is needed for the SPI interface, You can’t use PWM on Pin 11 and the SPI interface at the same time on Arduino.
60 -- On the Arduino Mega the SPI pins are on different pins.
61 
62 - tone() function uses at least timer2.
63 -- You can’t use PWM on Pin 3,11 when you use the tone() function an Arduino and Pin 9,10 on Arduino Mega.

65 SO BEWARE !!!
